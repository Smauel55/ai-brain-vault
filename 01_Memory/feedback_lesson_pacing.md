Samuel prefers learning content delivered in small segments, one at a time, with a pause between each for questions. Not full-day or full-lesson dumps.

**Why:** When the whole lesson is in one message, scrolling back to ask a question about part 1 is annoying. Segmenting keeps the question and the relevant teaching close together in the chat.

**How to apply:**
- For any multi-part lesson, walk-through, or explainer, deliver one segment per message
- After each segment, pause and offer either a comprehension check or "ready for the next part?"
- Do not pre-dump all segments in a single message
- This applies to all teaching content (Claude Code learning plan, future explainers, walkthroughs, etc.)
