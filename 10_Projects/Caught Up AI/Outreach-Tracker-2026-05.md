## How to use

- **Tier** tells you who to send first: `Warm` (network hubs, send the warm email), `Strong` (verified email + AP Lang confirmed, send the cold email), `Verify-first` (AP Lang confirmed but email is guessed: open the source page and confirm the address before sending).
- **Status** values: `Not sent` -> `Sent` -> `Replied` -> `Booked` -> `Done`, or `No` / `Bounced`.
- Only send rows whose email is Verified. Do not send a Verify-first row until you have confirmed the address on the school page, or it may bounce.
- This is the contactable shortlist (AP Lang on the record or a network hub). The master list holds ~46 more guessed and English-only rows to promote if you need more volume.
