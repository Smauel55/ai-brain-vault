### Research methodology + limitations

WebSearch and web_fetch were the only tools available. web_fetch enforces a strict URL-provenance restriction (only URLs already in user messages or returned by prior web_fetches are retrievable). That blocked direct fetching of nearly every school's staff page. As a workaround, Verified rows depended on email addresses appearing in WebSearch result snippets quoting the underlying page. Guessed rows used the email-format pattern confirmed on at least one Verified colleague at the same school/district.

CPS schools (Payton, Northside, Whitney Young, Lane Tech, Jones, Lincoln Park, Lindblom, Brooks) tend not to display emails on the public staff page — Lane Tech is the exception because its English department maintains a separate Google Sites page that does.

Suburban districts (Hinsdale 86, dist113, ETHS, Naperville 203, IPSD 204, CUSD 200, Glenbrook 225, d125, OPRF) generally publish teacher emails on subject-area pages or teacher home pages, which is why Verified rows cluster there.

All Guessed rows should be re-verified against the named source URL before any outreach. Several teachers (notably Hitzeman at Naperville North, Kotty at Fenwick) lead departments but teach AP Literature or American Studies rather than AP Language specifically — the "(AP Lang unconfirmed)" tag in Notes is the signal for that.

IATE leadership page (https://iateonline.org/) is by far the highest-yield single source for verified Illinois English-teacher emails (7 emails displayed verbatim as mailto: links).
